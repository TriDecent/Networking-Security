Tran Vu Anh Tri

We have discussed the legitimacy or legal status of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam (DRV). The ‘loser’ Republic of Vietnam (RVN) perhaps rarely had the opportunity to defend its legitimacy. Until now, one of the most deeply ingrained impressions Vietnamese students have about the RVN is that it was a puppet government, with puppet military and administration, set up by the United States. However, this perspective somewhat oversimplifies modern Vietnamese history from the viewpoint of international law.
